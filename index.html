About Us

LUCKYSHIPS is an independent ship sale & purchase brokerage serving clients across the global maritime industry. We assist in matching buyers and sellers of commercial vessels — including tankers, bulk carriers, offshore units, and specialized tonnage. Our team supports every stage of the transaction, from initial enquiry to closing, ensuring smooth communication and transparent execution. Whether you are looking to sell a vessel discreetly or source a suitable unit for purchase, we are here to guide you through the process.
